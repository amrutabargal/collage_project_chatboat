## Citation Format

When citing these references in your paper, use the following format:

**For Journal Articles:**
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, Volume(Issue), Pages. DOI/URL

**For Conference Papers:**
Author, A. A., et al. (Year). Title of paper. *Conference Name*, Pages.

**For Books:**
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of Book*. Publisher.

**For Web Resources:**
Organization. (Year). Title. URL
